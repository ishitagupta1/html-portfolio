The Best Movies According to Ishita

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spider-Man Across The Spider-Verse

My most recent favourite movie. Obsessed with the idea of parallel universe and the amazing soundtrack.

The Social Network

Loved the focus on social media and entrepreneurship as well as a great source to understand the origins of Facebook.

My Neighbour Totoro

Creates a magical and whimsical world filled with enchanting forest spirits.
